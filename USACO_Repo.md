### A Competitive Programming Repository and GitBook in C++

My USACO repository, <a href="https://github.com/Aryansh-S/USACO" target="_blank">open source on GitHub</a>, implements algorithms, data structures, and problems in competitive programming, with special emphasis on the United States Computing Olympiad (USACO). Extending the repository, my GitBook, <a href="https://aryansh.gitbook.io/informatics-notes/" target="_blank">available here</a>, provides detailed advice, setups, and concept explanations for prospective competitive programmers.
