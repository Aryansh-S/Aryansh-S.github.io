### A One Dark Color Scheme

My repository fastdark.vim, <a href="https://github.com/Aryansh-S/fastdark.vim" target="_blank">open source on GitHub</a>, is a minimalist take on the One Dark color scheme for Neovim and Vim, best experienced with Neovim Treesitter or Vim Polyglot.
